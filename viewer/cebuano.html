Cebuano-aware · experimental

Search how a medicine may be read—even when it is misread

This tool compares the official spelling with internal rule-based reading paths and approximate letter or syllable alignment. It can tolerate selected Bisaya/Cebuano-influenced readings as well as some missing, added or transposed parts of an unfamiliar medicine name. The official drug name is never changed, and internal localized forms are hidden from the user.

Important limitation: The rules are pronunciation hypotheses informed by current project research. They have not been validated as a complete model of Cebuano speakers and should only generate candidates for human review.
